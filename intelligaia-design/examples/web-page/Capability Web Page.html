Case Study — Logistics

A route network that re-plans itself.

Meridian Freight asked us to take a routing-optimisation proof-of-concept from a data-science notebook to a system dispatchers actually rely on every day.

The engagement

Three stages, one dispatcher-facing outcome.

Workshops Labs Studio Live

Discovery

Four weeks mapping dispatcher workflows, fleet constraints, and the existing routing model's failure modes.

Prototype

A re-planning engine validated against six months of historical dispatch data before a single line shipped to production.

Production

Full-stack build: routing service, dispatcher console, and the monitoring layer that tells the team when to trust it.

Route network, re-planned in real time
The first version we shipped didn't win on accuracy. It won because dispatchers could see why the system re-routed a truck, not just that it had.

What shipped

  • A re-planning engine that recomputes routes against live fleet position, not a nightly batch.
  • A dispatcher console that explains every re-route in plain language, not just a confidence score.
  • A monitoring layer that pages the team before dispatchers notice something is wrong.
  • A rollout plan that moved one depot at a time, so the fallback path was always live.
41%
Outcome

reduction in late deliveries in the first quarter after the re-planning engine reached full fleet coverage.
