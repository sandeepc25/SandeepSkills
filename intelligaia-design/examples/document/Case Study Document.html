Most firms deliver a technical build. We own the outcome — design and engineering working together so the reconciliation engine actually runs the monthly close.

The Workshop surfaced a narrow, well-understood problem: three source ledgers, one manual spreadsheet, and a controller re-keying totals every month-end. Labs validated a rules-plus-ML matching engine against eighteen months of historical statements before Studio took it into production.

Every unmatched line now has a reason attached to it. The controllers stopped re-checking the engine's work within the first close cycle.
